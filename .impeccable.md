## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are startup founders, hiring managers, and product teams reviewing Sahil for design-engineering roles or collaborations. They scan quickly, judge credibility in seconds, and need clear proof of shipped work.

### Brand Personality
Intentional, sharp, and founder-minded. Tone should feel confident, high-agency, and craft-driven without hype.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial-tech portfolio with asymmetry, strong typography, restrained accents, and clear content hierarchy. Dark-first with a polished light mode. Avoid generic SaaS gradients, glass cards, and template-like card grids.

### Design Principles
1. Prove, do not claim: every section should show outcomes, systems thinking, or shipped artifacts.
2. Hierarchy first: make the most important information obvious in 3 seconds.
3. Motion with purpose: use animation for orientation and feedback, not decoration.
4. Keep contrast high and interactions keyboard-visible.
5. Maintain one coherent visual language across hero, projects, and contact.
